Anticipation Builds for Somali Night
SSA is looking ahead to Somali Night on April 3rd, one of the organization’s most anticipated events of the year. It brings together students, families, alumni, and community members for an evening celebrating Somali culture, talent, and pride. The event highlights performances, community spirit, and the vibrancy of Somali identity on campus.
Somali Night will take place on April 3rd and celebrate Somali culture, talent, and community pride.
