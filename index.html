Our Purpose

A space where Somali excellence
meets academic ambition.

The Somali Student Association at the University of Minnesota exists to celebrate our shared heritage, uplift our community, and create a home away from home for every Somali student who walks through our doors. We are more than a club — we are a legacy in progress, rooted in culture and driven by purpose.

Signature Event

Somali Night

Our flagship annual celebration is a full-scale cultural production — featuring theatrical performances, traditional fashion, original music, spoken word, and dance choreography that draws hundreds from across campus and beyond.

About SSA

Grounded in culture.
Driven by excellence.

The Somali Student Association at the University of Minnesota is an organization built on the belief that heritage and academic excellence are not in conflict — they are complementary. We organize, we celebrate, we support, and we lead.

From cultural events to academic resources, community dinners to large-scale productions, SSA has been a home for Somali students at UMN for over a decade.

Heritage

Preserving and celebrating Somali culture through art, performance, and community.

Excellence

Holding ourselves to the highest standard in everything we produce.

Community

Building lasting bonds among Somali students across generations.

Leadership

Developing the next generation of Somali leaders on campus and beyond.
